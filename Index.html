What are your operating hours?

Our standard hours are 6am-10pm daily. Premium members have 24/7 access with their key fob.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes! We have certified personal trainers available for one-on-one sessions. Premium members receive 2 free sessions with their membership.

What types of group classes do you offer?

We offer a variety of classes including HIIT, yoga, spin, pilates, strength training, and more. Our class schedule changes seasonally.

Is there a minimum contract length?

No! All our memberships are month-to-month with no long-term commitment required.

Do you have locker rooms with showers?

Yes, we have fully equipped locker rooms with showers, towels, and personal care products available for your convenience.
